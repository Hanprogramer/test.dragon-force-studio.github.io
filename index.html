Our mission is to create quality and unique addons, texture packs, skins,
and maps to provide to Minecraft Bedrock players.
As a team we aim to expand the potential of Minecraft to make it a more enjoyable experience.
This is the official Dragon Force Studio website for downloadable content for Minecraft: Bedrock Edition (previously known as Pocket Edition).
Here you will find high quality add-ons, maps, skins and everything is free to download.

(Website started on March 30, 2020.)

Dragon Force Studio are in no affiliation with Mojang Studio.
We just love Minecraft and want to offer Bedrock players some great content(add-ons, maps, skins)
to make the game more exciting and more enjoyable to play!
Our vision is to expand Dragon Force Studio and become widely known among add-on creators and users on Minecraft Bedrock.
We aim to achieve this goal by working together as a team and pouring dedication and passion into our projects.
